Periods Shouldn’t Stop Potential

At Pearls of Hope Foundation, we believe that no girl should miss school or hide in shame because of her period. Yet, for many young girls and teen moms in underserved communities, access to basic menstrual hygiene products remains out of reach.

That’s why we’re launching our Free Pad Distribution Program — a mission to ensure that every girl can manage her period with dignity, comfort, and confidence.

We are reaching out to schools, community centers, and local shelters to distribute sanitary pads to those who need them most. This initiative not only provides essential hygiene products but also includes education on menstrual health, breaking harmful taboos, and promoting self-respect.

Activities include:

  • Keep girls in school during their periods
  • Educate communities about menstrual health and hygiene
  • Break the silence and stigma around menstruation

A pad might seem small, but to her, it can mean everything.

Together, let’s create a world where a period is never a problem.
