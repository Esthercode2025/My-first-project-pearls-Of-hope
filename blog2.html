Period Poverty: What It Is and Why It Matters

Every month, millions of girls around the world face a silent challenge one that keeps them out of school, limits their opportunities, and damages their self-esteem. This challenge is called period poverty.

Period poverty refers to the lack of access to menstrual products, hygiene education, and safe, sanitary facilities. It affects not just physical health, but emotional and mental well-being.

At Pearls of Hope Foundation, we believe no girl should feel ashamed of her body or be held back by her biology. That’s why menstrual hygiene is one of our core advocacy areas. We distribute free pads, teach menstrual health education, and create safe spaces for open, stigma-free conversations.

Join us. End the shame. End period poverty.
